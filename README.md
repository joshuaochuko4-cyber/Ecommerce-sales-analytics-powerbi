---

## 📌 Project Overview
Designed and developed an interactive E-Commerce Sales Analytics 
Dashboard in Power BI to evaluate revenue performance, customer 
purchasing behavior, product contribution, and geographical sales 
distribution.

---

## ❓ Problem Statement
Businesses dealing with large volumes of transactional sales data 
often struggle to extract clear, actionable insights. This project 
transforms raw sales data into an executive-level analytical model 
that supports data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objective
To build a structured analytical model that provides stakeholders 
with real-time visibility into key performance metrics and business 
trends.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Data Visualization Best Practices

---

## 🔧 Data Preparation & Cleaning
- Cleaned raw sales dataset using Power Query
- Handled missing values and removed inconsistent records
- Corrected data types for numerical and date columns
- Created time intelligence columns (Year & Month) for trend analysis

---

## 📐 Data Modeling & Measures
- Built DAX measures: Total Revenue, Total Quantity, Total Orders,
  and Average Unit Price
- Ensured accurate KPI calculations using proper aggregation logic
- Designed a scalable data model to support dynamic reporting

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features
- **KPI Cards** — Revenue ($105M), Quantity (6M), Avg Unit Price ($17.6)
- **Year-over-Year indicators** — percentage growth vs previous year
- **Monthly Sales Trend** — line chart for performance tracking
- **Customer Insights** — top spending customer segments
- **Top 5 Products** — by quantity sold
- **Geographical Sales Map** — country-level sales distribution
- **Interactive Slicers** — dynamic filtering for deeper analysis

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- Revenue grew **16.1%** compared to the previous year
- Sales volume reached **6M units** indicating strong market demand
- Sales are geographically concentrated in high-performing countries
  including Bangladesh, India, and Lithuania
- A small number of products (led by Cl at 2.4M units) drive a 
  significant portion of total sales volume

---

## 💼 Business Impact
The dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly assess performance, 
identify growth opportunities, monitor key metrics, and make 
strategic decisions based on real-time data insights.
